Adopt a Kitty

All cats at MKC are from Purrfect Companions of Norfolk Cat Rescue, a registered non-profit charitable organization operated by volunteers dedicated to cat rescue and adoption.

Our Cats

PCON cats are:

  • Spayed or neutered
  • Vaccinated
  • Microchipped and treated for flea prevention and deworming
  • Tested for FIV and tested NEGATIVE for Feleuk

The Facts About FIV and FeLV

The MKC residents are all adoption ready for a charitable adoption fee:

  • $250 per kitten or $400 for a two kitten adoption (under 1 year)
  • $175 per kitty or $300 for a two cat adoption (over 1 year)
  • $100 for FIV+ kitty or Senior Cat
  • Purebred Cat: Special Price – Please Inquire

Adoption Process

Before beginning the adoption process, it is required that you book a visit or drop-in to My Kitty Cafe (numbers permitting) to meet all of our PCON current resident kitties there.

Book a Visit

After you’ve met and interacted with a cat that you are interested in adopting, the next step is to submit the online Adoption Application. You cannot submit for a kitty you have just seen on the website. A visit is required or your application will not be considered.

Adoption Application

Purrfect Companions of Norfolk does not necessarily approve applications in order of receipt. Our goal is to match the needs and personalities of our rescues to an adopter, striving to place the right cat into the right home.

Once the best match has been selected, that applicant will be contacted. The final step in the adoption process is an adoption meeting at MKC with a PCON Adoptions Volunteer. Upon a successful meeting, the kitty will go home.
